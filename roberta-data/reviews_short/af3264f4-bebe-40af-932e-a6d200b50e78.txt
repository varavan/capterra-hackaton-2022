At AcelRx we went from a paper based system to MasterControl and there is no comparison!  Definitely made things so much better to keep control of documents.
MasterControl has enabled our business to automate processes and capture electronic training and document approvals saving time and money and empowering end users to take control over their documents and processes.
The document and Training management is excellent and easy to configure, deploy and implement. 
Having training tied to the SOP and linked to the Production Records for real time verification.
