CareerPlug offers Firehouse Restaurant Group an easy to use recruiting and applicant tracking system.  The built-in templates (e.g., messages, ratings, assessments, rating) ensure consistency of use by our hiring managers.  The hiring workflow makes it easy for first time hiring managers and experienced managers to know at what stage is each applicant and what actions need to be taken.  

We have received positive feedback from candidates and new employees who have gone through our hiring process with CareerPlug.  They are very impressed with the computer and mobile interface and the ease of use.
